## Similarities:
- Both about [[Callback]]s

## Differences
- [[Reactor]] is focused on [[Initiation Events]] - "Something (maybe a resource) is ready if you want to do work on it" - where as the [[Proactor]] is focuses on [[Completion Events]] - "The work is done"
